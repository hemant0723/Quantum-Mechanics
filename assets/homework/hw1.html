  1. Complete the proof that:
    \[ \langle p \rangle = \langle \hat{p}_{op} \rangle = \int dx \, \psi^* \, \frac{\hbar}{i}\frac{\partial \psi}{\partial x} \] and show that \[ \langle p \rangle - \langle p \rangle^* = 0. \]
  2. Definitions: \[ \Delta A = \sqrt{ \langle (A - \langle A \rangle)^2 \rangle } = \sqrt{\langle A^2 \rangle - \langle A \rangle^2}, \] \[ \Delta B = \sqrt{ \langle (B - \langle B \rangle)^2 \rangle } = \sqrt{\langle B^2 \rangle - \langle B \rangle^2}. \]
    Define: \[ \bar{A} = A - \langle A \rangle, \quad \bar{B} = B - \langle B \rangle. \]
    Use the Schwarz inequality: \[ (\Delta A \, \Delta B)^2 \geq |\langle \bar{A}\bar{B} \rangle|^2 \] to show that \[ \Delta p \, \Delta x \geq \frac{\hbar}{2}. \]
